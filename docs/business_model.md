# Clinic - Business Model & Product Objectives

## Product Overview

**Product Name:** Clinic
**Category:** Mobile CRM / Sales Pipeline Management
**Target Market:** Aesthetic Clinics & Beauty Service Providers

---

## Product Purpose

Clinic is a **mobile-first CRM** specifically designed for aesthetic clinics that generate leads through social media channels (Instagram and WhatsApp). The product focuses entirely on the **sales workflow**, not medical or clinical operations.

### Core Functions:
1. **Lead Capture** - Centralize inquiries from Instagram DMs and WhatsApp
2. **Pipeline Management** - Visual Kanban board to track lead status
3. **Follow-up Scheduling** - Never miss a potential sale with reminders
4. **Sales Conversion** - Guide leads from inquiry to closed deal
5. **Basic Reporting** - Track conversion rates and lead sources

---

## Customer Pain Points

### Primary Pain Points:

1. **No CRM Adoption**
   - Small clinics don't use traditional CRM tools
   - Enterprise solutions (Salesforce, HubSpot) are overwhelming and expensive
   - Staff lacks technical training for complex systems

2. **Chaotic Lead Tracking**
   - Leads arrive via Instagram DMs, WhatsApp, phone calls
   - Receptionists use WhatsApp chat history as "database"
   - No centralized view of all inquiries
   - Context gets lost in chat threads

3. **Lost Leads**
   - Inquiries slip through the cracks
   - No follow-up reminders
   - Staff forgets to respond to messages
   - Potential customers go to competitors

4. **No Pipeline Visibility**
   - Owners don't know how many leads are pending
   - No visibility into which stage leads are at
   - Cannot identify bottlenecks in sales process

5. **Zero Reporting**
   - No conversion rate tracking
   - Unknown lead source effectiveness
   - Cannot measure staff performance
   - No data-driven decision making

6. **Manual & Repetitive Work**
   - Copy-pasting price lists constantly
   - Retyping same responses to common questions
   - No templates for quick responses

## Key Differentiators

### vs. Not Using a CRM:
- See all leads in one place (not buried in chat apps)
- Never forget a follow-up (automated reminders)
- Know your numbers (conversion tracking)
- Professional lead management (no more chaos)

### vs. Traditional CRMs:
- Built for mobile (works from your phone)
- Designed for aesthetic clinics (not generic)
- Simple onboarding (no training needed)
- Affordable (priced for small businesses)
- WhatsApp/Instagram focused (where leads actually come from)

### vs. Generic Mobile Apps:
- Industry-specific features (treatment price lists)
- Pre-built Kanban stages for beauty sales
- Integration mindset (click-to-chat functionality)
